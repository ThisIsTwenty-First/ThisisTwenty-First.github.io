为何要探索自我Why explore the self

何为自我？这是许多人穷极一生所要去追求的答案，而从古至今，已经有无数先辈都思考过这个看似普遍大众，但若是能感受到它，却是一个让生活不再迷茫的方向。

What is ego? It is an answer that many people spend their lives seeking, and there have been countless forefathers from ancient times who have pondered this seemingly universal, but if one can feel it is a direction that makes life less confusing.

大学，这是从小就能听得到的大人们常说的生活“圣地”。对于一部分人，它是汲取无穷知识的地方；而对于另一部分而言，大学则是代表了逃脱舒适圈之后开始成为自己最为关键的转折点。

University, it’s the mecca of life that you can hear adults talking about from a young age. For some, it is a place of endless knowledge, while for others, university represents the most crucial turning point in escaping the comfort zone and starting to become who you are.

过了十八岁以后步入成年人的世界，我们所能够看到的、听到的都与之前会有很大的差别。而在这些差别之下，往往会面临不安、迷茫、纠结、顺从、冲突、斗争等等，而如何坚定的“做自己”，这也成为了人们最为纠结的一点。

When we turn 18 and enter the world of adulthood, what we can see and hear will be very different from before. And with all these differences, one is often faced with restlessness, confusion, entanglement, conformity, conflict, struggle and so on, and it becomes a point where people struggle the most with how to be determined to be themselves.

生活就好比塑料瓶一样容易被扭曲，我们对自我的认识无意识的大多依托于他人的评价，从而无法对自己产生正确的认知。想要去冲破那些无形的框架，但却总是会因为各种“前言”和“后语”而不敢行动，最后幡然醒悟，兜兜转转还停在原地。

Life is as easily distorted as a plastic bottle, and our perception of ourselves is unconsciously mostly dependent on the judgement of others, thus preventing us from developing a correct perception of ourselves. There is always a desire to break through those invisible frameworks, but one is always afraid to act because of all the preaching, and then one comes to the reality that one is still at the same place in a roundabout way.

都说最稳妥的便是走他人都走过的路，但那些荆棘之地也总要有人先与他人一步。二十出头，正是不怕险阻敢走的年纪，放眼望去，大家好像都没有去试错的勇气。有人随着大流在社会所默认的规则里慢慢变得沉默，而有的人则是在不停地被现实和远方拉扯着最终郁郁终生。

It is said that the safest thing to do is to take the path that others have taken, but someone has to be one step ahead of the others in those thorny places. In their early twenties, they are the age when they are not afraid to take risks and dare to go, and when they look around, they don’t seem to have the courage to go through trial and error. Some people slowly become silent in society’s default rules, while others are constantly pulled by reality and dreams to end up in depression.

平凡，自始自终都不是一个贬义词。在普通的世界里，做一个普通的人本没有错。

Ordinary, since the beginning, has never been a derogatory term. There is nothing wrong with being an ordinary person in an ordinary world.

接受平凡并不普通的走向平凡，在墨守成规的生活里活出自己的不平凡，这才我们最终想要传达给大家的意义。

Accepting that the ordinary and not the commonplace goes to the ordinary, and living one’s own extraordinary life in a life of conformity, this is what we ultimately want to convey to you.
