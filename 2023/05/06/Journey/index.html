心路历程Journey

《贰拾壹》的团队成员们在作品的主题上，也曾做过多次的组内头脑风暴，确定一个大家都认可的主题是非常重要的一环。通过五位成员所提出的不同想法，最后在思想的碰撞下，我们发现了大家所关注的交叉点，是以进行了初步的主题内容的确立（如图示）。

The team members of Twenty-One have also done many in-group brainstorming sessions on the theme of the work, and it is very important to determine a theme that everyone agrees on. Through the different ideas put forward by the five members, and finally under the collision of ideas, we found the intersection of everyone’s concerns, and carried out the preliminary establishment of the theme content (as shown in the figure).

在作品的呈现方式上，我们所选择的是装置艺术，通过有效的选择、改造、排列等，以令其演绎出新的展示个体或群体，从而表现出”场地+材料+情感”的综合展示艺术。我们希望通过作品的展出能够加强观赏者与场地的联系，使观赏者与之产生共鸣，从而减轻思想负担。当然，想法都是美好的，但是其能够真正达到我们想要的结果，还是需要一而再再而三的去进行完善。

In the way of presenting the works, we choose installation art, through effective selection, transformation, arrangement, etc., so as to make it interpret a new display individual or group, so as to express the comprehensive display art of “site + material + emotion”. We hope that the exhibition of the work will strengthen the connection between the viewer and the site, and that the viewer will be able to relate to it and thus lighten the burden of thought. Of course, ideas are beautiful, but to really achieve the results we want, it still needs to be perfected again and again.

所谓“共鸣”，即是在某个事物上能够找到同自身相同的关注点。

The so-called resonance is to be able to find the same focus on something as oneself.

而我们作为作品的设计者，如果作品本身同我们自己的关联并不是特别强，那么结果可想而知。我们总是去主动亦或是被动的接收外界的信息，但关于我们自己是否想要、是否需要却没有好好的进行过思考。是以，我们想要回归到自我，从自己出发，这一次认真的听听内心想要的、想表达的到底是什么。

And we, as designers of the work, if the work itself is not particularly related to ourselves, then the result can be imagined. We always actively or passively receive information from the outside world, but we don’t think hard about whether we want it or not. Therefore, we want to return to ourselves, start from ourselves, and this time listen carefully to what our hearts want and want to express.

在做这个项目的过程中，我们也遇到了许多困难，例如过程拍摄，材料组装等。但整体来说我们的思路也更加清晰了，从广撒网到逐渐学会了在广泛的灵感中找到一个有意义的延伸点，从仅仅听从别人的意见到能够清晰地表达自己的想法，从一开始不知所措到最后的意志坚定。

In the process of doing this project, we also encountered many difficulties, such as process shooting, material assembly, etc. But on the whole, our thinking has also become clearer, from casting a wide net to gradually learning to find a meaningful extension in a wide range of inspirations, from just listening to the opinions of others to being able to clearly express our own ideas, from being overwhelmed at the beginning to being determined at the end.

对我们来说，这不仅仅只是一次布展，这也是我们第一次与真实的自己进行深入交流。尽管过程很辛苦，但是远远不比我们所收获的万分之一。

For us, this is not just an exhibition set-up, it is also the first time we have an in-depth exchange with our true selves. Although the process is hard, it is far less than one ten-thousandth of what we gained.
