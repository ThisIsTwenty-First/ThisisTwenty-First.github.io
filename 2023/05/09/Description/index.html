作品简介Brief description of the work

作品名称：《贰拾壹》
The title of the work: Twenty-one

设计理念：寻找本我、发现自我、拥抱超我、成为我
Design concept: Find the Self, Discover the Self, Embrace the Superego, Become the I

设计说明：
Design instructions:

我们总是会在他人的言语和目光中对自己产生来回的质疑，在做自己和随大众之间来回摇摆，但在经历了无限的拉扯后最终留下的却是支零破碎的自己。

We will always question ourselves back and forth in the words and eyes of others, swinging back and forth between being ourselves and following the public, but after experiencing infinite pulling, what we end up leaving behind is a fragmented self.

性格，是促使一个人做任何一个决定的主要因素之一，而之所以我们会成为不同的人，其根本则是因为成长环境的不同所导致的。从出生开始，我们就受到了原生家庭对我们带来的无意识的影响，随着成长，社会因素也逐渐开始影响我们。

Personality is one of the main factors that motivate a person to make any decision, and the reason why we become different people is fundamentally due to the different growth environment.From birth, we are unconsciously influenced by our original family, and as we grow up, social factors gradually begin to affect us.

所以，如何通过一个东西来表达我们所经历的种种，这就成为了我们所去思考的地方。

So, how to express what we are experiencing through a thing, this becomes where we think.

每一次的欢喜、每一次的忧愁，其实都是我们所做是事情的结果。那么，什么可以成为选择的物质表现，在我们经过多次推敲后想到了“门”这个元素。

Every joy, every sorrow, is actually the result of what we do. So, what can be the material expression of choice, we think of the element “door” after many scrutinizes.

门，它们可以是一个家庭的缩影,每一次的开关都承载着不同的情感和不同的选择。门不仅仅是一个具体的事物，它也可以是一个虚拟的任意门。它的背后可以是我们每一个梦想，也可以是我们每一次的无奈。

Doors, they can be a microcosm of a family, each opening and closing carries different emotions and different choices. A door is not just a concrete thing, it can also be a virtual arbitrary door. Behind it can be our every dream, or our every helplessness.

它们是创造出你的家门，是未知世界的大门，也是你的心门。

They are the door that creates your home, the door to the unknown, and the door to your heart.

当然门不光是我们情感的外在表现，也可以作为我们的回忆，存在于我们心里的任何角落。

Of course, the door is not only an external expression of our emotions, but also can exist in any corner of our hearts as our memories.

而为何是二十一扇门，则是因为做此作品的我们是二十一岁，我们在这个年纪开始了探索自我的旅程，这是我们内心的灵魂本质，一切都是刚刚好的存在。

And why it is twenty-one doors is because we are twenty-one years old when we do this work, and at this age we begin our journey of self-exploration, which is the essence of our inner soul, and everything is just right.

但其实如果细数一遍会发现其实只有二十扇门矗立于此，那么第二十一扇又将会是在哪里呢？

But if you count it carefully, you will find that there are only twenty doors standing here, so where will the twenty-first door be?

你的每一个眼神，每一次互动其实也早就和其融为了一体。因为它们也代表了你的目的、你的选择、你的过去。

Every look in your eyes, every interaction with you, has long been integrated with it. Because they also represent your purpose, your choices, your past.
