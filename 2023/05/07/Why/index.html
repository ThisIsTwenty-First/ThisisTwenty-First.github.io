何为二十一What is twenty-one

有人说，寻找最真实的自己，这是一个老生常谈的话题，许多人在这个话题上经过、伫立、生根、发芽、结果，为什么还要去花时间进行探讨呢？这听起来是一个会让人止步不前的肯定句，但对于二十一岁的我们来说，这正是寻找自我、探索自身的开始。

It’s been said that finding your truest self is a topic of old conversation that many people have passed through, stood, taken root, sprouted and borne fruit on, so why bother taking the time to explore it? It sounds like an affirmation that would stop people in their tracks, but for us at twenty-one, it is the beginning of a search for ourselves and an exploration of ourselves.

为什么会有两个人做着相同的工作？为何大家都想要有物质保障的生活？这些从本质上来说，不也是重复着相同的动作吗？一个领域只有一人是万万不够的，三人成行、众人一心，
那么发现、探寻又有何不可呢？

Why are there two people doing the same job? Why do people want a life of material security? Aren’t these, in essence, the same actions over and over again? One person in a field is never enough， three people make a journey, many people make a journey. So what’s wrong with discovering and exploring?

“三十而立，四十而不惑，五十而知天命，六十而耳顺，七十而从心所欲，不逾矩。”孔夫子十五岁立志学习，而到了暮年才反思自己为学的一生。其实我们都是站在先人的肩膀上看待事物，每个事情的道理都是经过了反反复复的论证、推翻而后才成为了现在的所认为的“正确的”真理。正是因为有孔夫子、孟夫子等先儒率先进行“人”的探索，这才能有如今自小学习大道的我们。

“Thirty and standing, forty without confusion, fifty and knowing the destiny of heaven, sixty and obedient, seventy and obedient to the heart, and not overstepping the rules.” Confucius was fifteen years old and determined to study, but it was only in his twilight years that he reflected on his life as a student. In fact, we all look at things from the shoulders of our ancestors, and the truth of everything has been repeatedly argued and overturned before becoming the correct truth that we now believe. It is precisely because Confucius, Mencius and other Confucians took the lead in exploring people that we can learn the road since childhood.

幼时那些晦涩难懂的文字，如今也能品出其中一两分的道理。仅仅是二十而已，少年最不去缺的，便是去寻找远方的勇气。二十一岁的我们有着十七八时所没有的自由，有更多能够自己掌握的选择，有着稚嫩的成熟，在成年的世界里莽撞前行，去试探、会质疑、在迷茫，我们想要在二十一岁的年华里寻找最想要成为的自己。

Those obscure words from childhood can now taste one or two points of truth. It’s only twenty, and the last thing a teenager lacks is the courage to find a distant place. At the age of twenty-one, we have the freedom that we did not have when we were seventeen or eighteen, we have more choices that we can control ourselves, we have a mature maturity, we are reckless in the adult world, to tempt, to question, to be confused, we want to find the self we want to be in the age of twenty-one.

就如村上春树在《挪威的森林》里写到：“我一点也没做好二十岁的准备，挺纳闷的，就像是谁从背后推了我一下，我曾以为二十多岁，是鲜花，是远方和浩瀚的大海，那时的我会去看山河大海，落日余晖，但实际上我还在寻找自己，像是闯进了一团迷雾，乐观不起来，也没有勇气一颓到底。”

As Haruki Murakami wrote in “Norwegian Forest”, I was not ready to be twenty years old at all, quite puzzled, like who pushed me from behind, I thought that in my twenties, it was flowers, it was the distance and the vast sea, at that time I would go to see the mountains and rivers and the sea, the sunset, but in fact I was still looking for myself, like breaking into a fog, optimistic, and not having the courage to fall to the end.

曾有过许多的“心灵鸡汤”，但真正直击我们内心的，真的又有几个？他人的终究是他人的，同样的一句话语，从他人口中说出和从自己心里涌出，那种直抒胸臆的情感无人能及。

There have been many chicken soups for the soul, but how many really hit our hearts? After all, the other person’s is someone else’s, and the same words come out of his mouth and from his own heart, and the kind of straightforward emotion that no one can match.

我们总是被环境所裹挟，为他人而喜怒哀乐，是否还在倾听来自灵魂的声音。在独自成长的日子里，或许爱折腾且三分钟热度，但那又怎样呢？在最美好最炙热的二十多岁里，无论走向那个方向都是前进，我们的人生有无限的可能性。

We are always caught up in circumstances, happy and sad for others, whether we are still listening to the voice of the soul. In the days of growing up alone, maybe love to toss and three minutes of heat, but so what? In the best and hottest twenties, no matter which direction is forward, our life has infinite possibilities.

“二十一”是来自灵魂深处的重量，也是我们最真实的情绪体现，愿我们每个人的二十一岁都曾追光而遇，都能沐光而行！

Twenty-one for us is a weight from the depths of the soul, but also our truest emotional embodiment, may each of us have chased the light at the age of twenty-one, and can walk in the light!

“你是你自己。”

You’re yourself.

“你，是你自己。”

You, being yourself.

也只能是你自己。

You can only be yourself.
